Life atPathshala

Pathshala Nepal brings together children and adults of wide variety of ethnic groups and nationalities in inter-cultural network/harmony while celebrating the diversity of our school population. We also seek to forge among parents, students, teaching and non-teaching staff. This unified and caring community exhibits mutual respect and understanding where each individual feels welcomed and valued. We believe that through consistent application of these principles and ideals; a learning community will be nurtured in which rational, caring and intellectually discerning students will develop.
